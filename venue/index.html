Conference Venue

The conference will be held at at Building of Electrical Engineering (no. 16 in the map) Department of Computer Science and Engineering, National Sun Yat-sen University

Address: No. 70, Lienhai Rd., Kaohsiung 80424 Taiwan, R.O.C.

Access Map

The venue is conveniently accessible from Kaohsiung City MRT stations in Hamasing Station via Orange Line. Below are the recommended routes for reaching the venue.

From Hamasing Station (via the Kaohsiung MRT Orange Line)

  • Take the Orange Line For a direct connection to the venue, it is recommended to take the Bus Orange no.1 to university administration building. (Buses run every 30 minutes) From this station, it is about 900-meter walk (15 minutes) through the university tunnel entrance to the university administration building. Then, 6 minutes walk to Building of Electrical Engineering.

From Airport, Railway, Taiwan High Speed Railway

  • Take the Kaohsiung MRT Red Line to Formosa Boulevard Station. Transfer to the Kaohsiung MRT Orange Line stop at the Terminal stations—From Hamasing Station.

For additional directions, please visit The National Sun Yat-sen University campus map.
